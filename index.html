Player 1: roll the dice. Each time you roll, your total is added up. If you manage to score 20 or more you win, but if you roll a 1, your score is 0 and Player 2 plays! If you are satisfied with your score, press hold to pass to Player 2. Player 2: you must now try and score 20, but if you throw a 1, your score is 0 and play goes back to Player 1. First player to win chosen number (choose between 1 and 20) of rounds wins! Otherwise defaults to 4.
